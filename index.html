Greetings friends. My name is Patrick Aloysius Ghugerty-Hammerschmidt III. You can call me Pat. Having grown up in the beautiful Pacific Southeast, Salmon was a staple of my diet, as it is for any good Pacsoutheasterner. We are a seafairing people after all. My grandmother Ghugerty-Hammerschmidt handed down a multitude of old-world recipes, many of which my family still prepares to this very day.

My favorite childhood recipe from the family cookbook is the very recipe we use today for Pat's Original Salmon Cookies. What started as a tiny, one-family operation has grown into a multi-national Salmon Cookie empire the likes of which the world has never seen. Thanks Grandma!

Pat's Salmon Cookies are made from the finest ingredients available. We start with wild-caught Chinook Salmon from the chilly Pacific Southeast coast and fresh-picked sugar cookies from our own private cookie farm. Our proprietary Salmoncookiecrumb-o-nator2000 ™ does the rest!

Oh, and don't forget to check out our great products at Pat's Cookie Shack!
